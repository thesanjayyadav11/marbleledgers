1. Scope of this Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • The Marble Ledgers website
  • The Marble Ledgers cloud software platform
  • Mobile or web applications operated by the Company
  • Customer support and communications

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

Information that identifies an individual user, including:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company name
  • Business address
  • Login credentials

Business Data

Information created or uploaded by users within the platform including:

  • Invoices
  • Customer records
  • Supplier information
  • Inventory data
  • Accounting entries
  • Financial transactions

This data remains under the control of the customer.

Technical and Usage Data

We may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Operating system
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage logs
  • Platform interaction data

3. How We Use Information

We use collected data for purposes including:

  • Providing and operating the Service
  • Creating and managing user accounts
  • Processing subscription payments
  • Providing customer support
  • Improving platform functionality
  • Monitoring security and preventing fraud
  • Communicating service notifications and updates

4. Payment Processing

Subscription payments may be processed by third-party payment processors including Razorpay.

Marble Ledgers does not directly collect or store credit card information.

Payment information is processed according to the security and privacy practices of the payment processor.

5. Customer Data Ownership

All business data entered into the Marble Ledgers platform remains the property of the customer.

Marble Ledgers processes such data solely for the purpose of delivering the Service and maintaining platform functionality.

6. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Where applicable under the General Data Protection Regulation, we process personal data based on one or more of the following legal grounds:

  • Performance of a contract
  • Legitimate business interests
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • User consent where required

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

We may share information with:

  • Payment processing providers
  • Cloud hosting and infrastructure providers
  • Analytics service providers
  • Security monitoring providers
  • Government or regulatory authorities where legally required

These providers are required to maintain confidentiality and protect personal data.

8. International Data Transfers

Because Marble Ledgers operates globally, information may be transferred and processed in countries other than the user's country of residence.

We implement appropriate safeguards to ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect personal information, including:

  • Encrypted communication protocols
  • Secure authentication systems
  • Infrastructure monitoring
  • Restricted data access policies
Security Note

However, no system can guarantee absolute security. We continuously update our security practices to protect your data.

10. Data Retention

We retain personal and business data only as long as necessary for:

  • Providing the Service
  • Maintaining customer accounts
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Resolving disputes
  • Enforcing contractual agreements

Users may request deletion of their personal data subject to legal obligations.

11. User Privacy Rights

Depending on the user's location, individuals may have rights including:

Access to personal data
Correction of inaccurate data
Deletion of personal data
Restriction of data processing
Data portability
Objection to processing

These rights may apply under privacy laws including the California Consumer Privacy Act and other international regulations. Requests to exercise privacy rights may be submitted through our support contact.

12. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website and platform may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain login sessions
  • Analyze platform usage
  • Enhance platform performance

Users may manage cookie preferences through browser settings.

13. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a security breach involving personal data, Marble Ledgers will take reasonable steps to notify affected users and relevant regulatory authorities where required by applicable law.

14. Children's Privacy

The Marble Ledgers platform is intended for business use and is not directed toward individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

15. Third-Party Links

Our website or platform may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of such third-party websites.

16. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or service features. Updated versions will be published on the Marble Ledgers website. Continued use of the Service after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
